Crystal Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game!

There are four crystals below. When you click on a crystal, you will add a random amount of points to your total score.

You can win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If you go above the random number, you will lose.

The value of the crystals is hidden from you until you click it. You must remember the number since it will stay hidden!

Each time the game restarts, the crystal values will reset as well!
